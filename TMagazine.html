Web design and development (HTML, CSS, JavaScript) of the digital magazine “Times” This satirical digital magazine endeavors to shift the anthropocentric perspective on the world, weaving tales and reporting news from the realm of inanimate objects. These ‚things‘ encompass a diverse array: from lightbulbs and doorbells to cell-phones and laptops, each discreetly but visibly fulfilling their functions.

Meanwhile, within the unseen digital domain, where data swirls imperceptibly to human senses, the atmosphere is charged with rapidly-moving data packets and the content that constitutes inter-machine communication. The magazine also invites contemplation on how nonhuman entities might influence human experiences and perceive their own existence.

The magazine is written in two languages: English and Leet Speak 1337.

Under supervision of Verena Panholzer @studio.es @viskom_linz
